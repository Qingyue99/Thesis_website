Boston as Case Stduy

Food is a way to express ourselves. Time, social interaction, emotion and ethics all influence the place we eat, the food we choose to eat, and how we connect to others through food. All the food-related choices continue to influence the way we represent our social values and how we want to be seen by others in the citylife. Through daily observation in Boston, I document the scenes of how people interact with food-related environments from typical weekday to weekends.
